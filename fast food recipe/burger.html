Burgers Recipe

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments

Instructions

  1. Mix the ground beef, onion, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Form the mixture into patties.
  3. Grill the patties for 4-5 minutes on each side.
  4. Toast the burger buns on the grill.
  5. Assemble the burgers with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and condiments.
