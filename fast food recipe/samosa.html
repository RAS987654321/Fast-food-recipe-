Samosa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, water, and salt to make the dough.
  2. Prepare the filling with potatoes, peas, and spices.
  3. Roll out the dough and cut into circles.
  4. Fill with the potato mixture and fold to seal.
  5. Deep-fry until golden brown.
