Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 200g tomato sauce
  • 200g mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Spread the tomato sauce over the pizza dough.
  3. Top with mozzarella cheese and your favorite toppings.
  4. Bake in the oven for 15-20 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
  5. Let cool slightly before slicing and serving.
